Getting around

Inter-Island Ferries

Public speedboat ferries connect the inhabited islands daily. We reserve your seats and coordinate pickups — here's the full schedule and what to expect on board.

Routes & Schedule

Santa Cruz ↔ Isabela

Santa Cruz → Isabela 7:00 AM · 3:00 PM
Isabela → Santa Cruz 6:00 AM · 3:00 PM

Santa Cruz ↔ San Cristóbal

Santa Cruz → San Cristóbal 7:00 AM · 3:00 PM
San Cristóbal → Santa Cruz 7:00 AM · 3:00 PM

📍 There's no direct ferry between San Cristóbal and Isabela — connect through Santa Cruz on both legs.

  • 🧑‍✈️ Traveler assistance
  • 🦺 Life jackets
  • ⚓ Safe maritime transport

What to Expect on Board

  • 🚤Open-water speedboat, ~38 passengers
  • ⏱️2 hours of navigation per crossing
  • 💺Reclining seats & professional crew
  • 🚻Onboard restrooms for all passengers

Good to Know Before You Book

  • Arrive early: It's mandatory to check in at the dock at least 30 minutes before departure.
  • Dock access fee: A $1 per-person fee is charged at the dock when boarding.
  • Luggage limit: 23 kg included; excess baggage is charged at $10.
  • Isabela entry tax: A separate $10 municipal entry tax applies when arriving on Isabela.
